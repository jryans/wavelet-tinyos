Commands

  • result_t init (void) Initialize the LEDs; among other things, initialization turns them all off.

  • result_t redOn (void) Turn the red LED on.

  • result_t redOff (void) Turn the red LED off.

  • result_t redToggle (void) Toggle the red LED.

  • result_t greenOn (void) Turn the green LED on.

  • result_t greenOff (void) Turn the green LED off.

  • result_t greenToggle (void) Toggle the green LED.

  • result_t yellowOn (void) Turn the yellow LED on.

  • result_t yellowOff (void) Turn the yellow LED off.

  • result_t yellowToggle (void) Toggle the yellow LED.

  • uint8_t get (void) Get current Leds information

  • result_t set (uint8_t value) Set Leds to a specified value
